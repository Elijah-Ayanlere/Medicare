General health check

At Medicare, we offer comprehensive general health checks to help you maintain optimal health and detect any potential issues early. Our thorough examinations and advanced diagnostic tools ensure you receive a complete picture of your health.

  • Comprehensive Evaluations: Our health checks include a detailed assessment of your overall health, covering all major systems and vital signs.
  • Early Detection: Regular health checks help in the early detection of potential health problems, allowing for timely intervention and treatment.
  • Personalized Advice: Based on your health check results, our medical professionals provide personalized advice and recommendations to maintain or improve your health.

Diagnosis & treatment cancer

At Medicare, we are committed to providing comprehensive care for cancer patients, focusing on accurate diagnosis and effective treatment. Our multidisciplinary team of oncologists, surgeons, and support staff ensures personalized care to achieve the best possible outcomes.

  • Early Detection: We emphasize early detection through advanced screening methods and diagnostic tests to detect cancer at its earliest stages.
  • Multidisciplinary Approach: Our team of specialists collaborates to develop personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's specific type and stage of cancer.
  • Advanced Treatment Options: We offer a range of advanced treatment options, including surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, and targeted therapies.
  • Supportive Care: Alongside treatment, we provide comprehensive supportive care services to manage side effects, improve quality of life, and support overall well-being.
  • Clinical Trials: As part of our commitment to innovation, we offer access to clinical trials and research studies to explore new treatment options and improve outcomes for cancer patients.

Treatment of dermatitis

At Medicare, we offer effective treatment options for dermatitis, focusing on relieving symptoms and improving skin health. Our dermatologists utilize a personalized approach to address various forms of dermatitis, ensuring optimal care and patient satisfaction.

  • Diagnosis: Accurate diagnosis is crucial for determining the type and severity of dermatitis, which guides the treatment plan.
  • Topical Treatments: We provide topical medications such as corticosteroids, moisturizers, and barrier creams to alleviate inflammation and discomfort.
  • Avoidance of Triggers: Educating patients about triggers and irritants that exacerbate dermatitis helps in managing and preventing flare-ups.
  • Phototherapy: For severe cases, phototherapy (light therapy) may be recommended to reduce inflammation and promote healing.
  • Lifestyle Modifications: Advising on skincare routines, diet adjustments, and lifestyle changes to support skin health and manage dermatitis effectively.
